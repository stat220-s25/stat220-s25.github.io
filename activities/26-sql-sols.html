Stretch question

Find Viola Davis’s full filmography, in chronological order. Include each movie’s title, production_year, and the name of the character that she played.

SELECT title.title, title.production_year, char_name.name
FROM cast_info 
JOIN title  ON cast_info.movie_id = title.id
JOIN char_name ON char_name.id = cast_info.person_role_id
WHERE cast_info.person_id = 2977372
  AND title.kind_id = 1
  AND cast_info.role_id = 2
ORDER BY production_year;
